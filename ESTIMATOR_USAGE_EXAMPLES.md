## 🎯 Key Points About Detection

### What Works Well
✅ Technology names (React, Vue, Angular, etc.)
✅ Framework names (Django, Flask, Laravel, etc.)
✅ Feature names (authentication, payment, etc.)
✅ Industry terms (ecommerce, CRM, ERP, blockchain)
✅ Action words (build, develop, integrate, deploy)

### Limitations
⚠️ Very vague descriptions may miss details
⚠️ Misspelled words won't match (e.g., "Ract" instead of "React")
⚠️ Informal language may be missed (e.g., "app" vs "application")
⚠️ Only English keywords supported currently

### Tips for Best Results
1. **Be specific**: "React Native" better than "mobile"
2. **Mention platforms**: "iOS and Android" catches both
3. **Include features explicitly**: "with authentication and payments"
4. **Name technologies**: "Node.js", "MongoDB", "AWS" if using them
5. **Describe requirements clearly**: "real-time updates", "multi-language", etc.

## 🔍 How to Use It

### Step 1: Open the Estimator
Navigate to the Project Estimator section

### Step 2: Choose How to Input

**Option A: Manual Selection** (Traditional)
- Select Budget from dropdown
- Select Timeline
- Select Industry
- Toggle Services and Features manually

**Option B: AI Description** (New Feature!)
- Start typing in the "📝 Or Describe Your Services & Features" textarea
- Watch as services and features get detected automatically
- See colored badges appear showing what was matched

### Step 3: Apply Detected Services
- Click "✓ Apply These Services & Features" button
- All detected items are applied to the estimator
- Cost updates automatically
- "🤖 AI-Detected Services" badge appears in Summary

### Step 4: Refine if Needed
- Manually add/remove services or features
- Adjust Budget, Timeline, Industry
- See cost update in real-time

### Step 5: Get Quote
- Review summary and timeline
- Click "Request Custom Quote" to send inquiry
- Our team will review and confirm details

## 💰 Cost Calculation Order

The estimator calculates costs in this order:

1. **Base Budget Tier Cost** (₦600K - ₦28M)
2. **+ Service Complexity** (Tier 1-6, ₦500K - ₦2.8M per service)
3. **+ Feature Costs** (₦520K - ₦650K per feature, with bulk discount)
4. **+ Scope Bonus** (₦0 - ₦8M based on feature count)
5. **× Timeline Multiplier** (0.88x - 1.35x)
6. **× Industry Multiplier** (0.92x - 1.2x)
7. **= Final NGN Estimate**
8. **÷ Exchange Rate** = Estimate in other currency
